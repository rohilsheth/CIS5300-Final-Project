# Simple Baseline
Our simple baseline uses a dataset of words that are labeled with their associated genders. Each word is labeled as male, female, or neutral. For each song, the simple baseline processes all words in the lyrics and counts the number of words that are labeled as male and the number of words that are labeled as female. It then calculates the proportion of all words that are male and female. A song is classified as sexist if the absolute difference between the male and female words is greater than 0.1.
